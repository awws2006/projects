Who we are?

We are a small Jordanian project specialized in 3D printing using FDM printers. We print all types of models (graduation projects, figures, machine parts, toys, etc.)

When did we start


in 2023 after the founder had the idea of the business and took the first steps , HE bought his first 3d printer , and started the company slicenprint

What can we do?

We specialize in converting 3D designs into objects professionally. so if there was a broken part of a machine, we print it. If you have a graduation project, we print it. If you have an idea, we print it.

Our mission

Spreading awareness about 3D printing as not many people know about it, so creating a community that uses 3D printing in their daily lives to make it easier is our greatest hope.
